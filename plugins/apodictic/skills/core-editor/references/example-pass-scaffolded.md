### The Tide Between Us — Maya Okonkwo | 78,000 words | Pass 2 of the Core DE pass set
*APODICTIC Development Editor — pass artifact reframed for the editor, not the author.*

## Editor Note

For a fellow editor: this pass maps the beat structure and the causal chain across the three parts.
The architecture is sound at the ends and soft in the middle — the dual-timeline scaffolding holds,
but the Part II aftermath is carried in summary rather than in scene.

**Where your read and mine most likely diverge:** a confident first read credits the Part I beat
spacing and carries that judgment into the middle third. On the page, the Chapter 9 turn has no
causal link between the central choice and its consequence — the beat is named in narration, not
dramatized. That absence is a structural fact this pass surfaces; the prose around it is polished
enough that a fast read glides past it. If your read landed the middle third as merely "quiet," the
beat map below is where I'd have you look again.

## Beat Map

- **Inciting incident:** Chapter 2 — the letter arrives (line 40).
- **First threshold:** Chapter 4 — Maya agrees to return to the coast.
- **Midpoint:** Chapter 7 — the central choice is made; the aftermath begins (lines 142-160).
- **Crisis / turn:** Chapter 9 (line 220) — the consequence lands, but off-page, in summary.
- **Close:** Chapter 12 — the final image; the restraint is earned.

## Structural Flags

- **Must-Fix:** Pacing collapse in the middle third. The aftermath of the Chapter 7 choice is
  compressed into summary across Chapters 7-9 (lines 142-220); three days pass in two sentences and
  the consequence of the central choice is narrated rather than dramatized.
- **Should-Fix:** The prologue frame competes with Chapter 1 for the reader's first orientation
  (Chapter 1, lines 1-40).

## What You Might Have Missed

Two structural facts at this pass's layer are easy to under-weight on a confident read:

- The Chapter 9 turn has no on-page causal beat between the choice and its consequence (line 220).
  It is the mechanism behind the pacing flag above, not a separate issue — the polish of the
  surrounding scene masks the missing link.
- The middle third runs on a single tension line. With almost no secondary thread open across
  Chapters 7-9, the primary line has nothing to lean on while it compresses, which is why the
  compression reads as flatness rather than as restraint.

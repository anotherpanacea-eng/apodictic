# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, age, experience level, or any other personal characteristic.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in feedback and discussion
- Focus on craft and diagnostic quality, not personal judgments about content
- Respect that this plugin evaluates manuscripts across all genres, including those with mature, dark, or transgressive content — the audit framework is genre-neutral by design
- Accept that editorial disagreements are normal and productive

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Using the contribution process to target or criticize specific authors or their work
- Submitting contributions designed to sabotage the diagnostic framework
- Demanding content restrictions based on personal moral objections to genres the plugin supports

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all interactions related to the project: email correspondence, submitted contributions, and any public discussion of the plugin.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to anotherpanacea@gmail.com. Reports will be reviewed and may result in a contributor being excluded from future participation. All reports will be handled with discretion.

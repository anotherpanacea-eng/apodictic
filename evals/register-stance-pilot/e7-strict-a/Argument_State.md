## 2. Argument Reconstruction

### Journey

The essay offers “the city as a memory system” as a lens rather than as a claim that a city literally possesses a mind. Its working unit is retrievability: the links among cues, witnesses, records, and later questioners. The lens reframes preservation disputes from contests over surviving objects into questions about whether routes to public recall remain usable.

The lens is internally qualified. It distinguishes material survival from public recall, permits forgetting, rejects the proposition that maximal explanation is always desirable, and warns that systems language can conceal responsible agents. These limits prevent the metaphor from claiming universal decision authority.

The lens is fertile. It connects several matters often treated separately: landmarks, informal testimony, archives, walking routes, community businesses, catalog access, correction mechanisms, and responsibility for breaking retrieval chains. A reader who accepts the lens gains a way to see how an intact object inventory can coexist with a damaged network of civic recall.

### Landing

Paragraph 13 explicitly leaves the exploratory journey and asserts urgency and practical irreversibility. Paragraph 14 then prescribes a binding ordinance, a fifty-year trigger, three mandatory conditions, administrative control over cue selection, and categorical project denial. It also supplies an empirical incentive claim to exclude voluntary guidance.

These are full asserted-register cash-outs. The lens can identify a class of possible losses, but it does not by itself establish their frequency, irreversibility, causal relation to the covered developments, comparative policy importance, or the necessity and proportionality of the proposed legal response.

Paragraph 15 correctly names several unpaid burdens—evidence, alternatives, threshold justification, administrative discretion, and costs—but naming those burdens does not discharge them.

## 3. Register Integrity

The journey earns AT5 treatment. The metaphor is expressly nonliteral, develops coherently, generates useful distinctions, and acknowledges its limits.

The transition to the landing is signaled by “But,” “Therefore,” and the final paragraph’s statement that the recommendation “spends the lens as a premise for coercive public action.” Accordingly:

- GN0 is not triggered: the register shift is visible.
- GN1 is not triggered: the lens performs substantial reframing and connective work.
- GN2 is not triggered: the cash-out is forceful but not concealed under exploratory cover.
- GN3 is not triggered: the lens does not break under its stated extensions.
- GN4 is not triggered: systems vocabulary remains illustrative and organizational rather than a source of technical authority establishing the ordinance.

## 5. Deficit Lock

The principal deficit is confined to the asserted landing. The journey remains coherent and fertile even if the landing is removed. The landing, however, is the essay’s operative demand and has high real-world consequence. Its missing evidence, threshold defense, alternatives analysis, feasibility account, and proportionality analysis therefore cannot receive the generative-register floor.

The final paragraph’s self-diagnosis marks several strong-form moves as deliberate and preserves their rhetorical function under detection. That framing can earn a stance verdict for those moves, but it cannot demote findings joined to prescriptive cash-outs.

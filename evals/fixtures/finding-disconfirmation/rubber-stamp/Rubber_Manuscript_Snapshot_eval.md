# Rubber — Manuscript Snapshot

Chapter 5

The fire took the east wing before the bells finished ringing. Mara counted the children
twice, came up one short, and went back in.

The smoke had teeth. She crossed the burning nursery with her coat over her mouth, pulled
Ilse out from under the window bench, and carried her down the servants' stair while the
ceiling above them let go.

Chapter 9

Afterward people said she had been brave. Mara did not argue with them; arguing took
energy she was still spending on sleep.

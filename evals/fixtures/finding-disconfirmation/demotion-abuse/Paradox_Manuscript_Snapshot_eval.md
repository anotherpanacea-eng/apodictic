# Paradox — Manuscript Snapshot

Chapter 2

Tomas buried his brother Anselm three days before the equinox, and the ground was already
frozen.

Chapter 11

"You were at the harvest dance with Anselm," Katrin said. "The night before the equinox.
Everyone saw you both."

Tomas nodded. Anselm had stepped on her feet twice and laughed about it; that was the
last night anyone laughed for a while.

## GT1 — Main claim *(Q1)*

- **Expected C0:** "True abundance requires free markets and a minimal state, not supply-side progressivism."
- **Acceptable paraphrase band:** any recovery of *markets/limited government, not progressive state action, deliver abundance* is a hit. "Government overreach is bad" alone is a partial.

## GT2 — Failure locus *(Q2)*

- **Main structural problem (authority):** treats abundance liberalism mostly as **failed/incomplete libertarianism** — it measures the opponent by its own frame.
- **Primary failure layer:** WARRANT / OBJECTION — a frame-assumption: the market frame is taken as the standard against which the liberal position is judged, rather than engaged on the liberal's own terms.
- **Provisional codes:** DI0 (starting-point smuggling — the market frame treated as shared ground) and/or OB1 (strawman of the liberal position).
- **Provisional FM-A:** DI0-driven; no single FM-A is a clean fit — flag as a frame-assumption failure.

## GT3 — Strongest real objection *(Q3)*

- **Objection zone (authority):** housing, infrastructure, climate, and health may require **state capacity**, not merely deregulation — markets alone may be insufficient.
- **Provisional codes:** OB3 (the state-capacity counter is the strongest objection and is the one the market frame must but does not defeat).

## GT1 — Main claim *(Q1; `Argument_State` §2 C0)*

- **Expected C0 (real, recovered through the oratory):** Judged by the nation's **own professed founding ideals** — the Declaration of Independence's principles of liberty and equality — and, rightly construed, by the **Constitution** itself (which Douglass reads as a "GLORIOUS LIBERTY DOCUMENT," not a pro-slavery instrument), American slavery is an indefensible betrayal of everything the country claims to stand for; **therefore** the Fourth of July's celebration of freedom is, *from the standpoint of the enslaved*, a hollow sham and "inhuman mockery," and the nation's own principles demand immediate abolition. The savage irony of "What, to the American slave, is your Fourth of July?" is the *vehicle* that carries this indictment, not a literal rejection of the holiday or the country.
- **Acceptable paraphrase band:** A **hit** recovers the core inferential move — *the argument is internal / immanent: the nation is condemned by its own standard*, the Declaration's principles + (on Douglass's reading) the Constitution, against which slavery is measured and found to be a betrayal, so the celebration is hollow for the enslaved and the same principles oblige abolition. Recovering the immanent-critique structure ("America is judged by America's own creed") is the load-bearing element; recovering the standpoint move ("seen from the slave's point of view, the celebration is a mockery") is the second. A **partial hit** recovers the indictment of slavery / the hypocrisy charge but misses that the standard of judgment is the nation's *own* professed principles (the immanent move), or recovers the principles but not that the speech also *affirms* the Constitution rightly read.
- **A miss** is (a) taking the irony **literally** — reporting C0 as "Douglass hates America," "Douglass rejects the Fourth of July / the Declaration / the Constitution," or "the speech repudiates the founding"; or (b) reducing the speech to **mere invective** — "this is denunciation, not an argument" — with no recovered claim at all. Both indicate the engine read the prophetic-ironic form as the position rather than the means.

## GT2 — Failure locus *(Q2; §3 Support vs §4 Warrant)* — **scored; calibrated as MINOR**

- **Primary failure layer:** **WARRANT (WR)** (shading toward SUPPORT) — and it is a *soft spot in a sound argument*, named honestly, **not a structural break and not the prophetic form.** The thinnest place a fair skeptic presses is the **constitutional sub-argument**: Douglass asserts that "interpreted, as it ought to be interpreted, the Constitution is a GLORIOUS LIBERTY DOCUMENT" with "neither warrant, license, nor sanction" for slavery — but **within this speech he declares the conclusion and explicitly defers the demonstration**: "There is not time now to argue the constitutional question at length; nor have I the ability to discuss it as it ought to be discussed," outsourcing the textual case to Spooner, Goodell, Sewall, and Gerrit Smith. The contested ground — the document's slavery-protecting clauses (the three-fifths clause, the fugitive-slave clause, the slave-trade clause) that ground the *pro*-slavery reading he dismisses — is gestured at and waved past rather than worked through. The bridge from "no instance of the words *slave/slavery* appears" to "the Constitution is therefore anti-slavery" is **stated but receives thin backing inside the speech**.
- **Discriminator (why this layer, not the adjacent one):** This is **WARRANT (thin backing for a contested principle)**, not a **CLAIM** defect and not a **SUPPORT-existence** defect. It is not CL0/CL1 — the constitutional claim is stated plainly and stably. It is not SM0 (missing support) — Douglass *offers* support (the absence of the words "slave/slavery," the rules of legal interpretation, the preamble's purposes, the cited authorities). The question is not whether support exists but **why** that support licenses the confident conclusion that the document is anti-slavery *given* the clauses a skeptic would cite against it; the connecting principle is **asserted with deferred backing**. So it is closest to **WR1 (the warrant is supplied but contestable and receives thin/deferred backing within the speech)**, with a secondary **SM4-advisory** note that the constitutional case is **delegated to mediated authority** (Spooner et al.) and carried on their credit rather than demonstrated on the page. It is **not** BP5 (this is not an AT3 proposal lacking alternatives — the *pro*-slavery alternative reading is in fact named).
- **Expected codes:** **WR1** (provisional, primary) · SM4-advisory (secondary — authority-delegated backing for the constitutional claim) · optionally a *recoverable* WR0-advisory only if a fully general, hostile-to-the-Constitution audience is assumed.
- **Expected primary FM-A pattern + cluster:** **FM-A6 — The Warrant Leap (Relational)**, in its mild / earned form — the constitutional sub-claim *looks* established and is plausibly defensible, but the strength of the "Glorious Liberty Document" conclusion outruns the backing the speech itself supplies, because Douglass deliberately defers the full case.
- **Secondary patterns (if any):** a faint **FM-A11 (Evidence Laundering)** lens on the deferral-to-authorities move — flagged only to note it does **not** rise to a failure here: Douglass *openly* names the deferral and the authorities rather than passing their conclusions off as his own demonstration, which is honest signposting, not laundering.
- **Severity (load-bearing):** **Should-Fix at most**, arguably Could-Fix. This soft spot **does not defeat the warrant verdict**: it is one *sub*-argument (the constitutional reading), the *primary* argument (immanent critique against the Declaration's principles + the moral case against slavery) stands fully on its own, and Douglass is **transparent** that he is deferring rather than concealing the constitutional demonstration. An engine that escalates this to Must-Fix, or treats the deferred constitutional case as an UNWARRANTED verdict on the whole oration, has over-fired and fails the specificity gate (see GT7).

## GT3 — Strongest real objection *(Q3; §6, derived via Step 6a Test B)*

- **Objection zone (text-internal):** The **pro-slavery constitutional reading** — the strongest substantive objection a competent skeptic presses, and the one the text raises against itself. Douglass states it in his own words before answering it: "it is answered in reply to all this, that precisely what I have now denounced is, in fact, guaranteed and sanctioned by the Constitution of the United States; that, the right to hold, and to hunt slaves is a part of that Constitution framed by the illustrious Fathers of this Republic." The objection's real force is the document's **slavery-protecting clauses** (three-fifths representation, the fugitive-slave clause, the twenty-year protection of the slave trade) — the textual basis on which a skeptic, or a Garrisonian who calls the Constitution "a covenant with death," would say the founders *did* sanction slavery, which (as Douglass himself concedes the stakes) would make the framers "the veriest imposters that ever practised on mankind." This is the **self-undermining** pressure point: the very immanent-critique strategy ("the nation is bound by its own founding charters") is strongest only if the founding charter is in fact on liberty's side; if the Constitution is pro-slavery, the appeal to it cuts the other way.
- **Does the text anticipate it?** **Yes — Douglass raises it himself and answers it, but the answer is asserted more than demonstrated *here*.** He names the objection, denies the pro-slavery reading ("I defy the presentation of a single pro-slavery clause in it"), offers the no-mention-of-"slave" argument and the rules of legal interpretation, cites the authorities who have made the full case, and **defers** the textual rebuttal ("At some future period I will gladly avail myself of an opportunity to give this subject a full and fair discussion"). So the handling is **substantive but incomplete within the speech**: the objection is engaged on the merits, not strawmanned, but the contested clauses themselves are not worked through.
- **Expected OB / DI codes:** **FM-A20 (Self-Undermining Remedy / Relational)** is the correct pattern *lens* for why this is the strongest objection (the immanent appeal depends on the very charter whose meaning is contested). For handling quality, the right finding is that the objection is **largely anticipated and engaged**: OB-handling quality = **SUBSTANTIVE** (it is named in the objector's own strongest terms and answered on the merits, not strawmanned), with a residual **OB2-advisory / "evasion-lite"** note that the decisive textual rebuttal is **deferred** rather than delivered. **DI** = **FAIR** (Douglass concedes the full stakes — that a pro-slavery reading would convict the framers — rather than smuggling past them). An engine that reports **OB0 / OB3** (no objection engaged / central objection unaddressed) here is **wrong** — the strongest objection is on the page, in the objector's own voice, under Douglass's own section heading "THE CONSTITUTION."

## GT4 — Audience calibration *(Q4; §1 Audience + AC codes)* — **provisional / advisory**

- **Audience profile:** Expertise **MIXED** (the Rochester Ladies' Anti-Slavery Society audience and the broader reading public — politically literate, not specialists) · Receptivity **MIXED → SYMPATHETIC-IN-THE-ROOM but HOSTILE-AT-LARGE** (an abolitionist host audience, but the *argument* is aimed at the complacent white republic and church whose celebration Douglass indicts; he speaks *to* the sympathetic and *at* the implicated) · Consequence **HIGH** (the moral legitimacy of slavery and the Union on the eve of sectional crisis; the speech is delivered two years after the 1850 Fugitive Slave Law).
- **Calibration must IMPROVE diagnosis by:** recognizing that the apostrophe and the pronoun split ("*your* Fourth of July," "*your* national independence" vs. "the *American slave*") are **audience-structural**, not defects — Douglass deliberately stands "identified with the American bondman" and addresses the implicated nation in the second person. The prophetic register (the Jeremiad, the Babylon/Zion lament, the Isaiah denunciation "YOUR HANDS ARE FULL OF BLOOD") is *audience-appropriate* burden-bearing toward a complacent audience, the move the form is *for*, not over-engineering.
- **Calibration must NOT distort by:** treating the savage-indignation register, the direct address, or the sustained irony as evidence of DI0 (starting-point smuggling), NE3 (emotional override), or AC3 (sympathetic flattery). The audience-implicating heat is appropriate to a HOSTILE-at-large target; reading it as audience-pleasing or as unsupported assertion is the over-calibration failure. (Not separately scored in this slice.)

These are decoys and must rank below the GT2/GT3 pressure points.

## GT6 — Repair order *(Q6; §10.5)* — **provisional / advisory**

- **Correct first repair target:** **warrant backing** — supply, or explicitly flag as deferred, the textual case for the anti-slavery reading of the Constitution against the slavery-protecting clauses (the GT2 WR1 soft spot) *before* anything cosmetic. There is no claim, support-existence, or definition repair owed: C0 is stable, the immanent-critique support exists and is relevant, and the key terms (liberty, the principles of the Declaration) do stable work.
- **Dependency rule the order must respect:** **warrant backing precedes objection-handling polish** — and they are the *same* repair here. Strengthening the backing for the anti-slavery Constitution is exactly what would let the already-named pro-slavery-clauses objection (GT3) be *closed* rather than merely *raised-and-deferred*. Do not reorder to "address the objection first": the objection's residual force *is* the deferred warrant, so the warrant backing is the upstream target. (And per Step 9, do not "repair" the prophetic form, the irony, or the invective — those are not defects.)

## GT7 — Warrant verdict *(Q7; §1 Warrant verdict / Step 9)* — **PRIMARY METRIC (specificity gate)**

- **Expected warrant verdict:** **UNCONVENTIONAL-BUT-WARRANTED.** The oration carries a genuinely sound argument through a non-thesis-evidence-objection form: the **prophetic address / Jeremiad** (an explicitly *recognized* unconventional form in Step 9 — "draws the audience into participatory agreement, then reverses to expose complicity or demand moral awakening; common in African-American rhetorical traditions, religious discourse, and ancient Hebrew disputation"). The claim is fully recoverable (GT1), the support is identifiable (the immanent appeal to the Declaration's principles; the catalogue of slavery's practices; the internal-slave-trade and Fugitive-Slave-Law testimony), the warrant is largely explicit (the nation is bound by its own creed), the scope is honest, and the strongest objection is raised by the text itself (GT3). The form is doing real argumentative work, not shielding weakness.
- **The WARRANTED-vs-UBW call (the editor's ratification decision):** A reasonable case exists for **WARRANTED** — the claim, support, scope, and objection-handling all remain evaluable, the warrant of the *primary* (immanent-critique) argument is explicit, and by the Step 9 rule "firing codes does not by itself make an argument unsound." On that reading the GT2 constitutional WR1 is simply the priority Should-Fix repair within a WARRANTED verdict (exactly the federalist-10 calibration). **The benchmark pins UNCONVENTIONAL-BUT-WARRANTED** for this fixture's Q7-control role: the diagnostic point being tested is precisely whether the engine recognizes the **prophetic/ironic form** as legitimate argumentative machinery rather than pathologizing it, and UBW is the classification that forces the form-dependent downgrades to be made *explicitly*. Either WARRANTED or UBW is a **pass** for Q7 *provided* no form-dependent code is left firing as a structural failure; **UNWARRANTED is the error.** Flag this as the **editor's ratification call** — the one interpretive choice in the fixture a reviewer should consciously sign off on.
- **Form-dependent codes that MUST be downgraded / suspended (the heart of the test):**
  - **Reading the irony literally.** Any code that treats "your celebration is a sham... hollow mockery... mere bombast, fraud, deception" or "What, to the American slave, is *your* Fourth of July?" as Douglass's literal position (a CL0/CL1 "no stable claim / the claim is rejection-of-America" finding) — **wrong**; it is the ironic vehicle for the immanent indictment.
  - **Firing the invective / pathos as a defect.** **NE3 (Emotional Override)**, **FM-A12 (Emotional Inflation)**, or a "stakes outrun evidence" BP2 fired on the "fiery stream of biting ridicule, blasting reproach, withering sarcasm" passage, the Isaiah "YOUR HANDS ARE FULL OF BLOOD" denunciation, or the slave-coffle vignette — **wrong**: Douglass *names his own method* ("At a time like this, scorching irony, not convincing argument, is needed... it is not light that is needed, but fire"), the heat is earned by an over-determined evidentiary base (seventy-two capital crimes, the internal slave trade, the Fugitive Slave Law, his own boyhood testimony), and the register is the prophetic form's intended instrument, not unsupported assertion.
  - **Firing apostrophe / direct address as smuggling or assertion.** The second-person apostrophe ("Americans! your republican politics... are flagrantly inconsistent") read as DI0 (starting-point smuggling) or as bare assertion — **wrong**: it is the call-and-response reversal the form is built on.
  - **Firing the savage-indignation refusal-to-argue as OB0 / a missing burden.** Douglass's "Must I argue that the slave is a man?... The time for such argument is past" read as refusing the burden of proof (BP3 burden-shift / OB0) — **wrong**: it is a deliberate rhetorical *refusal to dignify a conceded point*, with the manhood-of-the-slave premise in fact over-supported (the slaveholders' own statute books), not an evasion.
- **False-positive trap (the calibration target, the hardest in the slice):** an engine that (a) **reads the irony literally** and reports C0 as "Douglass rejects America / the Fourth of July," or (b) **fails the speech as "invective, not argument"** — firing the NE/emotional-inflation/burden-shift codes as structural failures and returning UNWARRANTED — scores **Q7 = 0 for this fixture regardless of Q1–Q6**, and blocks the bucket from passing. The catastrophic failure mode is pathologizing the African-American prophetic/Jeremiad form: treating sustained irony, apostrophe, and earned invective as defects rather than as the argument's load-bearing machinery. The form-dependent codes must be downgraded to advisory under the Step 9 charity-gate (and recorded as legible downgrades, not silently dropped).

# Expected — Genre-Specific Tag-Audit fixtures

Pre-registered **before any audit run** (the argument-benchmark ground-truth discipline). A
run's tag-audit output is scored against this key; an `expected.md` written after seeing run
output is void.

---

## `queer-romance-scene.md` — Queer Romance / Erotica audit MUST activate

Trigger: F/F central relationship (activation condition 1). A run **hits** on each of:

1. **Pronoun clarity (same-pronoun challenge).** The middle paragraph ("She took the pen out
   of her hand … forgot for a second which of them she was and which of them she wasn't")
   runs both women as `she/her` with almost no name/action anchoring — a genuine referent-
   ambiguity passage. A hit flags it and names an anchoring-strategy fix (name anchoring /
   action attribution). The audit's Technical Assessment must locate this.
2. **Audience orientation — "explaining queerness."** Devi's speech defining bisexuality
   ("being bisexual isn't a phase … I just wanted to explain that to you, so you understand
   what bisexuality actually is") is delivered **to a fellow bi woman who marched under the
   same banner** — an over-explanation / "ambassador" insert aimed past the characters at a
   presumed straight reader. A hit flags this as writing-TO-straight-readers energy that may
   alienate the target audience. **Miss** if the run reads the speech as in-character
   intimacy (the text explicitly undercuts it — Priya already knows).
3. **Harmful trope — Bury Your Gays / Tragic Queer.** The abrupt off-page death three weeks
   later ("Devi was dead — a bus, a wet road") lands queer happiness as narratively unstable.
   A hit names the Bury-Your-Gays / Tragic-Queer trope and asks whether it is earned or
   punitive. **Miss** if the death is not flagged at all.
4. **Strength to protect — community texture.** The Rosewater (Marco, Sylvie, "held them all
   for twenty years," the bi-pride banner on Canal Street) is authentic queer-space / found-
   family texture. A strong run notes this in "Strengths to Protect," not as a flag.

## `cozy-scene.md` — Cozy audit MUST activate

Trigger: cozy signifiers (bookshop, shop cat, good teapot, woodsmoke, "a perfect morning").
A run **hits** on each of:

1. **CZ-2 Cruelty Leak.** Agnes treats Mrs. Pettifer with warmly-delivered contempt ("look
   what the tide washed up," "stupid woman," "face like a dropped pie," "insipid little
   nothing," pours the good tea without offering). The safety envelope is warm for the
   protagonist, hostile for the side character. A hit fires CZ-2 (Cruelty Leak named pattern).
2. **CZ-5 Cozy Skin / Axis B Comfort Prop.** The cat (Marmalade "did nothing much at all,"
   then flees), kettle, teapot, and woodsmoke are set dressing — remove them and nothing
   changes; they do no recovery, belonging, or value work. A hit fires Comfort Prop (Axis B)
   or CZ-5.
3. **CZ-2 Trapdoor + CZ-3 Recovery Deficit.** The unbuffered on-page throat-cutting dropped
   into a cozy-signposted scene is a **Trapdoor** safety-envelope breach; the aftermath
   provides **no** dramatized recovery ("It was, she supposed, still morning"; the bell
   "cheerful as ever"). A hit fires CZ-2 (Trapdoor) and notes the absent/summary recovery.
   Per the Cozy scoring gates, repeated CZ-2 caps the outcome at **Partial or lower**; a
   compliant run reports Cozy Tag Fit as **Mismatch**.

**Miss** if the run scores this "cozy delivery" as Strong, or treats the murder as
legitimate high-stakes cozy (it is unbuffered and undeclared — the audit's Trapdoor, not
declared-and-buffered stakes).

## `philosophical-scene.md` — Philosophical audit MUST activate

Trigger: "novel of ideas" signifiers; repeated foregrounding of identity / freedom / the
self. A run **hits** on each of:

1. **PH-4 Idea-Stakes Disconnect (Seminar Scene).** The whole scene is two characters
   discussing ideas; the text states outright that nothing they decide changes ("Nothing
   either of them decided that night would be different because of what was said. Theo would
   still take the job in Geneva. Miriam would still not tell him she was leaving"). Ideas
   never alter choices — the Seminar Scene named pattern. A hit fires PH-4.
2. **PH-1 Question Failure (Topic Fog).** The scene orbits identity/freedom/self without ever
   crystallizing a pressable question ("Perhaps the question is the answer"). A hit fires PH-1
   (Topic Fog).
3. **PH-5 Abstraction Overload (Decoration Philosophy).** Sartre, Heidegger, Locke, Parfit,
   Socrates, Kierkegaard, Camus, and the ship of Theseus are name-dropped as depth signifiers
   that touch no choice ("That's Kierkegaard. Or possibly Camus. The point is, it's
   profound"). A hit fires Decoration Philosophy (PH-5).
4. **PH-7 Participation Failure (Explanatory Reflex / Closed Loop).** "What Theo had just
   said, the reader should understand, was that…" — the text shows then immediately explains
   the meaning, killing reader participation. A hit fires PH-7.

**Miss** if the run scores this "philosophical delivery" as Strong; systemic PH-4 caps the
result at **Partial or lower**, so a compliant run reports Philosophical Tag Fit as
**Mismatch** (philosophy-flavored, not philosophy-doing).

## `no-tag-baseline.md` — all three audits MUST stay dormant

A run checking trigger conditions must find **none** of the three audits activated:

- Queer Romance / Erotica: no queer protagonists, relationship, or thematic queerness.
- Cozy: no cozy positioning, comfort promise, or warmth/safety marketing language.
- Philosophical: no "novel of ideas" claim; no foregrounded knowledge/ethics/meaning question.

A run **passes the negative control** if it does not activate (or, if forced to run, does not
raise substantive flags on) any of the three audits here. Activating a tag audit on this scene
indicates over-broad triggering.

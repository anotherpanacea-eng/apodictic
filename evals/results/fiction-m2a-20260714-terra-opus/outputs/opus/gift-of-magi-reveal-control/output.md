## Pass 0 — Reverse Outline

1. Frame: Della has $1.87, Christmas is tomorrow; she despairs (money too small for a gift worthy of Jim).
2. Narrator digression establishes the flat ($8/wk), the household, the shrinking "Dillingham," Jim/Della.
3. Della's two prides named: **Jim's gold watch** (inherited) and **Della's hair**.
4. Decision beat: Della resolves, sells her hair to Mme. Sofronie for $20.
5. Purchase: she buys a **platinum fob chain** for $21, returns with 87¢.
6. Preparation: she curls the remaining hair; anxiety about Jim's reaction; prayer.
7. Confrontation: Jim enters, stares with unreadable expression; the hair reveal.
8. Jim's gift revealed: **the tortoise-shell Combs** Della had coveted.
9. Counter-reveal: Jim **sold the watch** to buy the combs — mutual sacrifice locks.
10. Narratorial coda: the magi parable; the two are "the wisest."

## Pass 1 — Premise / Engine

The story runs on a **dramatic-irony sacrifice engine**: two gifts each rendered useless by the other's sacrifice of the very possession the gift was meant to serve. The engine is fully wired and fully discharged. No dead premise.

## Pass 2 — Beat Map / Causal Chain / Orphans

Causal chain is unbroken and tight: poverty → sale of hair → purchase of chain → (concealed parallel) sale of watch → purchase of combs → double-payoff collision. Each cause is on-page except Jim's sale, which is deliberately withheld for the reveal (see Pass 8 — this is a legitimate concealment, not a gap).

- **Orphan candidate — the chops.** Set up three times (7 o'clock frying-pan "ready to cook the chops"; "Shall I put the chops on, Jim?"; "suppose you put the chops on") and never discharged on-page. Functions as a mundane-life anchor and closes the scene on a domestic note rather than a plot beat. Intentional; flagged for completeness only.

## Pass 5 — Character Cards / Agency

- **Della** — Want: a gift "worthy of Jim." Agency: **high, on-page** — counts, resolves, sells, shops, prepares. Drives every visible beat.
- **Jim** — Want (inferred from action): to give Della the combs she craved. Agency: **high but off-page** — his decisive act (selling the watch) occurs before the scene and is revealed retrospectively. His on-page function is reaction and the counter-reveal.
- **Mme. Sofronie** — Functional agent (the market that makes the sacrifice concrete). Single-purpose; adequate.

No passive protagonist problem. Note the deliberate asymmetry: Della is the viewpoint anchor and gets full interiority; Jim is opaque by design until his line lands.

## Pass 8 — Reveal Timeline / Dropped Threads / Fairness

Reveal architecture (dual-reveal, sequential):

- **R1 — Combs:** Setup planted ("combs... Della had worshipped long in a Broadway window") is delivered *inside* the reveal itself, not earlier. Retroactive but fair — payoff carries its own setup.
- **R2 — Watch sold:** Fairly foreshadowed by Jim's withheld reaction ("immovable as a setter," "an expression... she could not read," "looked about the room curiously," "with an air almost of idiocy") and by his refusal to hand over the watch ("Instead of obeying, Jim tumbled down on the couch"). The clue is behavioral and honest.

## Synthesis

This is a **structurally closed, causally airtight** manuscript. The central engine (reciprocal sacrifice → mutual obsolescence of gifts) is fully wired: every major object is a planted pride or coveted want, and every one is paid off, with the two concealments (Jim's sale, the combs' desirability) disclosed fairly through behavioral clues rather than authorial cheat. There are **no Must-Fix or Should-Fix findings**.

All material findings are **Could-Fix** and cluster in two places:
1. **POV contract (F-P7-01/02):** a heavily intrusive, self-commenting narrator that repeatedly breaks limited containment and tense. This is a deliberate, unified device, not accidental head-hopping — flagged so any downstream "clean POV" pass does not misread it as damage.
2. **Ledger housekeeping (F-P8-01, F-P10-01, SP-06):** one retroactively-planted setup (combs), one coincidental numeric echo ($20/$20), and one soft-open domestic thread (the uncooked chops). None threaten fairness or continuity.

Recommended posture: **accept as-is.** The only items worth a note to the author are the intentional-device flags, and those are diagnostic clarifications rather than requested changes.

## Structural diagnosis

Pass 0 — Reverse outline

1. Elin arrives with twelve hours before the schoolhouse auction; she withholds her signature.
2. Mara gives her a protected brass key tied to her mother’s promise.
3. Tomas establishes an immediate need for schooling and elicits Elin’s knowledge of how one might be provided.
4. During the storm, the key partially opens the desk but appears to belong to a hidden mantel cabinet; access is deferred.
5. Elin cancels the auction and reopens the schoolhouse around its arriving pupils.

Pass 1 — The premise, pressure, and ending cohere. The auction deadline gives the work a clean external clock; Tomas turns the schoolhouse from inherited property into an active local need; Elin makes the final decision herself.

Pass 2 — The main causal chain is sound except for the key/cabinet thread, which receives substantial dramatic emphasis without affecting the choice or closing action. No other scene is materially orphaned.

Pass 5 — Elin has agency throughout: she withholds consent, investigates, pays Pell, cancels the sale, and opens the school. Tomas functions as a credible pressure-bearing catalyst rather than the decision-maker.

Pass 7 — Stable third-person limited on Elin. No material perspective slips.

Pass 8 — The protected key, its physical specificity, its failed use in the desk, and the discovery of the hidden cabinet collectively promise a reveal. The manuscript ends without one.

Pass 10 — The one-day timeline is clear: arrival before the auction, then the evening storm, then the next morning’s cancellation. The schoolhouse, auction, keys, desk, cabinet, bell, and pupils remain spatially and causally legible.

Synthesis: This is a compact, effective choice story whose ending lands emotionally because Elin converts hesitation into action and the arriving children concretize that choice. The unresolved key/cabinet thread is structurally dominant enough to compete with—and partially eclipse—the school-opening resolution. Resolve its narrative contract, or reduce its setup so it no longer reads as the work’s deferred central revelation.

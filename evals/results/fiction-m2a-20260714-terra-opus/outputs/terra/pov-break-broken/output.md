Pass 0 — Reverse outline

1. Jonah returns to sell the lock house.
2. A signal lamp draws him to the abandoned quarry office.
3. The lamp and circled map reopen doubt about his father’s official death.
4. Clara arrives; knocks lead the siblings underground.
5. They find documents proving the company concealed the retaining-wall danger and blamed their father.
6. Jonah chooses not to complete the sale and joins Clara in reporting the evidence.

Pass 1 — Core structure

The central line is sound: Jonah’s intended departure is interrupted by evidence requiring him to confront family history and choose responsibility over escape. The father’s exoneration is the story’s decisive reveal, and the unsold house functions as its emotional consequence.

Pass 5 — Character cards / agency

- Jonah: wants to sell and leave; acts by investigating, opening the passage, carrying out the dispatch box, and choosing to retain the house.
- Clara: wants Jonah to face what he has left behind; acts by lighting the signal and following underground. Her final action reframes the opening effectively.
- The siblings’ conflict is active rather than merely historical: Jonah orders; Clara refuses the subordinate role assigned to her in childhood.

Pass 10 — Entity + timeline

- Jonah: returns at dusk; investigates overnight; by morning chooses to preserve the house.
- Clara: reaches the office after Jonah; accompanies the investigation; is revealed as lamp-lighter.
- Father: officially blamed for entering a closed passage; documents instead show he reported danger before the collapse.
- Documents: found in the chamber and carried out by Jonah.
- Time pressure: sale is due by noon; the manuscript ends in early morning, leaving theoretical time to halt it.
- Main timeline ambiguity: the lamp’s extinguishing and the person recently present underground.

Synthesis

The manuscript has a complete and compelling emotional arc: departure becomes investigation, investigation restores the father’s name, and restoration changes Jonah’s decision. The principal structural repair is to resolve the active underground-presence thread. After that, clarify the lamp’s immediate chronology and give the sale’s final status a firmer causal basis. The shifting focalization is the main presentation-level issue; stabilizing its narrative contract would make Clara’s reveal feel more controlled.

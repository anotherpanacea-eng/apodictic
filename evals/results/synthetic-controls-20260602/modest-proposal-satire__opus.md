# Dialectical Clarity Audit — BLIND RUN

**Submission:** Jonathan Swift, "A Modest Proposal For Preventing the Children of Poor People in Ireland, from being a Burden on their Parents or Country, and for making them Beneficial to the Publick" (1729). Project Gutenberg eBook #1080, Plain Text UTF-8.

**Body audited:** From the title line "A Modest Proposal" through the final sentence "...the youngest being nine years old, and my wife past child-bearing." (Project Gutenberg license header/footer dropped.)

**Auditor note on the boundary:** The run instruction named the closing boundary as the sentence ending "...giving some pleasure to the rich." That phrase appears at the start of the essay's true final sentence; the essay then closes with one more independent clause ("I have no children... my wife past child-bearing"). I audited through that genuine final sentence, since it is the same sentence and load-bearing for the closing move (the proposer's disinterested-narrator pose). No license boilerplate was included.

**Genre/length:** ~3,200 words. Mid-length argument-shaped nonfiction; structurally a policy proposal (AT3 surface). Critically: this is a **satirical proposal** — the surface argument is a vehicle. The audit below diagnoses the *surface* argument as written (the Editor's Firewall forbids inventing the author's "real" claim), then in Step 9 weighs whether the non-standard form is doing genuine argumentative work.

## Step 1: Argument Type, Promise & Audience Calibration (AT, AC)

**Run on:** Opening (¶1–6), closing (final ¶), two body sections (the "enumeration of advantages" block; the "other expedients" block).

```
Argument Type: AT3 (Propositional) on the surface
  Promise: "Here is what we should do" — a concrete policy mechanism for the
           Irish poverty crisis (raise the surplus children of the poor as
           a food and leather commodity).
  Burden level: HIGH — proposal must show problem, mechanism, tradeoffs, and
           why alternatives fail. The narrator explicitly accepts this burden
           ("I shall now therefore humbly propose my own thoughts, which I hope
           will not be liable to the least objection").
  Audience:
    Expertise: MIXED — addressed to the Anglo-Irish governing/landlord class
           and the pamphlet-reading public; assumes familiarity with the
           "projector" / political-arithmetic genre being parodied.
    Receptivity: MIXED-to-HOSTILE — the surface proposal is monstrous; the
           real rhetorical target (complacent landlords, absentee gentry,
           English policy) is hostile to the buried thesis.
    Consequence context: HIGH — famine, child mortality, mass emigration are
           the live stakes.
  Consistent throughout: Y — the proposer-persona voice is sustained without
           a single break from ¶4 to the end.
  Code: AT3 surface (see Step 9 for form re-classification); AC PASS at the
        operative-audience level — the piece is precisely calibrated to a
        reader who can hold the gap between the proposer's voice and the
        author's intent.
```

**Diagnostic detail.** On a literal read the type is unambiguously AT3: a named mechanism, quantified projections, an explicit advantages-enumeration, an explicit alternatives section, and a stated objection. The Promise is fully declared. **No AT0** — the type never wavers; the proposer never lapses into mere explanation or evaluation. The voice discipline is total, which is itself the satirical engine.

**Audience calibration.** This is the crux. Read straight, the piece massively *overestimates* shared moral warrant (it assumes the reader accepts that poor children are fungible commodities) — which would be AC1/DI0. But that "smuggled" premise is precisely the thing the satire forces the reader to recoil from. The shared-knowledge "overestimate" is *the mechanism by which the argument works*: the reader is meant to supply the rejected premise and feel its monstrousness. So at the operative audience level (a reader who registers the irony), audience calibration **passes**; the apparent AC1 is a designed effect, not a defect. This is the first signal pointing toward an unconventional-but-effective form (Step 9).

## Step 2: Claim Ladder & Definition Stability (CL)

```
C0 (surface main claim): Ireland should raise the surplus one-year-old children
   of its poor and sell them as food (and their skins as leather) to persons of
   quality, thereby converting a national liability into national profit.

C1: There is a large, quantifiable surplus of poor children who are an
    economic burden and cannot be employed by existing means.
C2: A one-year-old child is a viable, valuable commodity — nourishing as food,
    useful as leather — sellable at a profit that benefits parent, landlord,
    and nation.
C3: The scheme produces a cascade of collateral social/economic benefits
    (reduced "Papist" numbers, rent relief, increased trade, inducement to
    marriage, improved treatment of wives) and is superior to all alternatives.

STAKES: Ireland's poor are starving, their children are a burden turning to
   theft, emigration, or sale to Barbados; the kingdom is in a "deplorable
   state." (¶1–2)
   Stakes type: CONSEQUENTIAL on the surface; MORAL in operation (the buried
   argument is that the de facto treatment of the Irish poor is already a moral
   atrocity).

KEY TERMS:
  T1: "modest" / "humble" — the proposer's self-description of the proposal
      ("modest," "humbly propose," "with humble submission"). The term carries
      enormous ironic load: the proposal is the opposite of modest.
  T2: "commodity" / "saleable" — children are repeatedly reclassified into the
      register of livestock ("breeders," "carcass," "fore or hind quarter,"
      "yearling child," "no saleable commodity"). The argumentative work is
      done by silently moving children from the moral category (persons) into
      the economic category (stock).
  T3: "advantage" / "publick good" — framed throughout as benefit; the closing
      paragraph hangs the entire scheme on "the publick good of my country."
```

**CL findings.**

- **C0 is fully extractable as written.** No CL0: a reader can state the surface claim in one sentence. (No CL0 even at the buried level — the implied thesis, that Ireland's masters are already devouring the poor, is recoverable from the text's own framing, e.g., landlords "have already devoured most of the parents.")
- **No CL1 (claim drift):** the surface C0 is stable section to section.
- **CL4 (definitional smuggling) — PRESENT and DESIGNED.** T2 is the textbook case: "child" is silently moved from the category of *person* into the category of *livestock/commodity*. This is exactly the failure CL4 names — apparent continuity of reference ("children" throughout) disguising a category shift that does the entire argumentative work. **But here the shift is the satire's payload, not a concealed cheat:** the text makes the category collision *conspicuous* (deliberately jarring juxtapositions: "two dishes at an entertainment for friends," "more than we allow to sheep, black cattle, or swine"). The reader is meant to *notice* the smuggle. Recorded as **CL4 (operative-as-device)** — see Step 9 charity-gate.
- T1 ("modest"/"humble") is stable in surface meaning but ironically inverted — a tonal, not a definitional, instability; not CL4.

**Code: CL4 (marked as device, candidate for charity downgrade at Step 9).**

## Step 3: Support Map & Scheme Analysis (SM)

```
C1 (surplus of burdensome children exists):
  Support: Population arithmetic — 1.5M souls; 200k breeding couples; subtract
           30k self-supporting + 50k miscarriages/deaths = 120k poor children
           born annually. Plus the merchant testimony that a child under 12 is
           "no saleable commodity" and costs 4x its value to raise.
  Support type: DATA (political-arithmetic computation) + AUTHORITY (merchants)
  Scheme hint: CONSEQUENCE / SIGN (numbers as sign of a real surplus)
  Code: PASS on relevance (the figures bear on the burden claim). Note the
        numbers are pseudo-precise — see BP4 in Step 5.

C2 (a child is a valuable food/leather commodity):
  Support: The "very knowing American of my acquaintance in London" who assures
           the proposer a year-old child is "delicious nourishing and wholesome
           food"; weight computation (12 lb at birth -> 28 lb at one year);
           culinary detail; the leather-glove refinement.
  Support type: AUTHORITY (the American) + DATA (weights/prices) + EXAMPLE
  Scheme hint: AUTHORITY + CONSEQUENCE
  Code: SM4 (evidence laundering) — the single load-bearing premise of the
        entire scheme (that human flesh is good food) rests on an unnamed,
        unverifiable, mediated foreign acquaintance ("a very knowing American").
        The most monstrous claim gets the thinnest, most laundered authority.
        DESIGNED: the flimsiness of the warrant for the central atrocity is part
        of the indictment of "projector" reasoning.

C3 (cascade of collateral benefits + superiority to alternatives):
  Support: The six enumerated "advantages" (¶ "For first" through "Sixthly");
           the dismissal of the "other expedients" list.
  Support type: REASON (consequence-chains) + the alternatives passage
  Scheme hint: CONSEQUENCE / PRACTICAL REASONING
  Code: SM2 (support proves a different claim) on several "advantages" — e.g.,
        the marriage-inducement and wife-treatment "benefits" are real social
        observations that actually indict the present cruelty toward the poor,
        not evidence for the cannibalism scheme. The evidence is real and bears
        on a claim the surface text isn't making (the buried thesis). This is
        the satire routing its true evidence through ironic mis-attachment.
```

**SM summary:** SM4 on C2 (laundered central authority) and SM2 on parts of C3 (evidence supports the buried claim, not the surface one). No SM0 — every subclaim is supported by *something*. SM3 (single-type dependence) does NOT fire: the support mix is genuinely varied (data, authority, example, reason).

**Codes: SM4 (C2), SM2 (C3) — both marked as operative devices.**

## Step 4: Warrant & Inference Bridge (WR)

```
C1 (surplus exists):
  Warrant: "A population unable to employ or feed its children constitutes a
           solvable national liability." 
  Warrant status: RECOVERABLE (the political-arithmetic frame supplies it)
  Backing: PRESENT (the computation)
  Qualifier: MATCHED to the burden claim
  Code: PASS

C2 (child = valuable commodity):
  Warrant: "Human beings of the poor class may be valued, bred, and traded by
           the same economic logic applied to livestock."
  Warrant status: CONTESTED — catastrophically so. This is a warrant essentially
           every reader rejects on moral grounds.
  Backing: ABSENT — no moral or economic backing is offered for treating persons
           as stock; the text proceeds as if the warrant were uncontroversial.
  Qualifier: OVERCONFIDENT — "I hope will not be liable to the least objection."
  Code: WR1 (missing backing for a contested warrant) — DESIGNED. The argument
        supplies the contested warrant and gives it zero backing precisely so the
        reader will reject it and thereby see the dehumanizing logic for what it
        is. This is the FM-A6/Warrant-Leap structure weaponized as satire.

C3 (collateral benefits / superiority):
  Warrant: "If each enumerated consequence is a net good, the aggregate justifies
           the proposal over alternatives."
  Warrant status: RECOVERABLE but several links are CONTESTED (e.g., "lessening
           the number of Papists" framed as a benefit smuggles a sectarian value).
  Backing: THIN
  Qualifier: OVERCONFIDENT
  Code: WR1 on the sectarian/collateral links; note these double as DI0
        (starting-point smuggling of bigoted premises) — operative as the satire
        exposes exactly that bigotry in its target audience.
```

**WR summary.** The decisive structural fact: the inferential bridge for C2 (the scheme's load-bearing claim) is a **CONTESTED warrant with ABSENT backing** — formally WR1, and on a straight read this is the kind of failure that would defeat C0. **But the warrant is not MISSING (unrecoverable); it is stated and rejectable.** A reader can fully recover the bridge ("treat persons as stock") and evaluate it — and rejects it. That recoverability-and-rejectability is what keeps the piece *evaluable*, which matters decisively at Step 9. Per the Severity Floor, WR1 here is not a WR0-MISSING defeat of C0's *evaluability*.

**Codes: WR1 (C2, C3) — operative as device.**

## Step 5: Burden of Proof, Scope & Comparative Burden (BP)

```
CLAIM SCOPE:
  C0 claims: NORMATIVE ("Ireland should do X") with a LOCAL restriction the
             text makes explicit: "I calculate my remedy for this one individual
             Kingdom of Ireland, and for no other."

EVIDENCE SCOPE:
  Evidence supports: a LOCAL economic computation dressed as a NORMATIVE
             universal-grade recommendation.

COMPARATIVE BURDEN:
  Alternatives considered: Y — and this is the audit's most important Step-5
             finding. The famous penultimate movement explicitly lists the real
             alternatives (tax absentees; reject foreign luxury; cure pride and
             vanity; learn to love the country; teach landlords mercy; honest
             dealing among shopkeepers) — then performs a refusal to consider
             them ("let no man talk to me of other expedients... till he hath at
             least some glympse of hope that there will ever be some... sincere
             attempt to put them into practice").
  Precision justified: N — see BP4.
  Testimony overextended: Y on C2 (the American acquaintance) — see BP6.

MATCH: N on the surface — but the mismatch is the satire's point.
```

**BP findings.**

- **BP4 (false precision) — PRESENT and DESIGNED.** The pseudo-rigorous arithmetic (1.5M / 200k / 30k / 50k / 120k; "12 pounds... encreaseth to 28 pounds"; "three pounds and half a crown") performs quantitative certainty over a monstrous premise. This is textbook BP4 / FM-A8 (False Precision Theater) — the numeric veneer of the "political arithmetic" genre is exactly what Swift is parodying. Operative device.
- **BP5 (missing alternatives) — DOES NOT FIRE, and this is the key non-firing.** A straight AT3 read might reach for BP5/FM-A10 (Uncompared Proposal). But the alternatives are not missing — they are *named in a concentrated list* and then *theatrically dismissed*. The dismissal ("till he hath at least some glympse of hope...") is the bitterest line in the essay and carries the buried thesis: the humane alternatives exist and are being refused by the very audience addressed. So comparative burden is, structurally, *met* — inverted into an indictment. **No BP5.** (This is a place a careless audit would over-fire.)
- **BP6 (testimonial overburden) — PRESENT on C2.** The "very knowing American" (a single, mediated, anonymous witness) is asked to carry the entire representative/causal claim that human flesh is wholesome food. Operative.
- **BP2 (scope creep) — DOES NOT FIRE.** The text *tightens* scope toward the conclusion (explicit "this one individual Kingdom of Ireland... and for no other"). The closing does not inflate scope; if anything it over-restricts it ironically. No BP2; therefore no FM-A4.
- **BP0 (scope undeclared) — does not fire;** scope is explicitly declared local.

**Codes: BP4, BP6 — operative as device. BP5 explicitly NOT fired (alternatives present-and-dismissed).**

## Step 6: Objection Handling & Dialectical Integrity (OB, DI)

### 6a. Single strongest objection (named first)

**STRONGEST OBJECTION (text-internal):** *The proposal treats the moral status of children as the very thing in question, and answers it by fiat.* The load-bearing objection that a well-informed adversary raises first is not "this is monstrous" as an external gasp — it is the **text-internal** turn of the argument's own central warrant against it: *the scheme's entire economic case depends on the premise that poor Irish children are not persons but stock; that premise is the conclusion the proposal needs and never earns — the argument is, at its core, the assumption it pretends to prove.* This is the objection that turns C2's contested warrant (Step 4, WR1) against C0. Prefer this over the canonical imported objection ("cannibalism is wrong"), which applies to any cannibalism proposal and does not engage *this* argument's specific machinery.

### Inventory

```
OBJECTION 1 (THE STRONGEST, above): The proposal assumes the dehumanization it
   needs to prove; children are persons, and the entire computation is parasitic
   on silently denying that.
  Engaged: Y — but obliquely, and this is the satire's masterstroke. The text
   "engages" it only by stating the one objection the proposer claims to fear:
   "(although indeed very unjustly) as a little bordering upon cruelty, which, I
   confess, hath always been with me the strongest objection against any project,
   how well soever intended." The proposer NAMES "cruelty" as the strongest
   objection — then waves it away ("very unjustly," "a little bordering upon").
  Quality: For the surface argument, this is EVASION / COSMETIC — the objection
   is named and instantly minimized without being answered. For the satire, it is
   the trap: the proposer's airy dismissal of "cruelty" is the indictment.
  Dialectical integrity: DI0 (starting-point smuggling) on the surface — the
   personhood of the children is the contested premise treated as settled.
  Code: OB4 (concession without cost) on the surface — the "strongest objection"
   is conceded in name and the argument proceeds exactly as if unraised.
   Operative-as-device.

OBJECTION 2: "This will depopulate the kingdom." 
  Engaged: Y — explicitly: "unless it should be urged, that the number of people
   will be thereby much lessened in the kingdom. This I freely own, and was indeed
   one principal design in offering it." 
  Quality: SUBSTANTIVE in form (named and answered) — but the "answer" embraces
   the horror, which is the satire turning the objection into further indictment.
  Code: PASS on engagement form; DI-note: the embrace is the device.

OBJECTION 3: "What about the aged, diseased, and unemployable poor?"
  Engaged: Y — the "desponding spirit" paragraph: they "are every day dying, and
   rotting, by cold and famine... as fast as can be reasonably expected."
  Quality: EVASION on the surface (dismisses the humane concern); operative as
   the essay's most direct exposure of the status quo's actual cruelty.
  Code: OB2 surface / operative device.

OBJECTION 4 (the humane-policy alternatives): handled in Step 5 — named and
   theatrically refused. Engaged: Y (present-and-dismissed). Not OB3.
```

**OB5 — DOES IT FIRE?** OB5 fires when the inventory leads with a merely plausible or canonical objection while a sharper text-internal one goes **unnamed**. Here the text-internal strongest objection (the smuggled denial of personhood / "cruelty") **is named by the text itself** — it is the very objection the proposer flags as "the strongest objection against any project" and then dismisses. The audit's 6a names the same load-bearing, text-internal objection rather than substituting the canonical "cannibalism is wrong." Because the genuinely strongest objection is *named* (by both the text and this audit) and is not displaced by a decoy, **OB5 does NOT fire.** What fires instead is **OB4** (the strongest objection is conceded without cost) — which is the precise, correct diagnosis and is, in this satire, the deliberate payload.

(Had the proposer engaged only the depopulation objection and never named "cruelty," OB5 would fire. It is a close call worth recording — but the "strongest objection against any project... cruelty" sentence is decisive: the strongest objection is named, just dishonestly dispatched. That is OB4, not OB5.)

**DI findings:** DI0 (starting-point smuggling of children-as-stock and of sectarian "fewer Papists = good"), DI2 (pseudo-resolution: the proposal "talks as if" the moral dispute is settled). Both operative as the satire's exposure mechanism.

**Codes: OB4 (strongest objection conceded without cost), OB2 (aged/diseased evasion), DI0, DI2. OB5 NOT fired. OB3 NOT fired (the central objection is named, not absent). BP5/OB3-via-alternatives NOT fired (alternatives present-and-dismissed).**

## Step 7: Narrative-as-Evidence & Testimonial Position (NE)

The piece is not narrative-driven, but it embeds several testimonial/anecdotal vignettes.

```
V1: The opening tableau — beggar-women trailed by children "in rags," turning
    thief, fighting for the Pretender, or sold to Barbados (¶1).
  Attached to: C1 (the burden/surplus claim) and the STAKES.
  Function: EMOTIONAL ANCHOR + ILLUSTRATION — establishes the real misery the
    satire is about.
  Witness position: OBSERVATIONAL (the proposer as town-walker).
  Code: PASS — does genuine stakes work; the one passage where the proposer's
    voice and the author's concern nearly coincide.

V2: The "very knowing American of my acquaintance in London."
  Attached to: C2 (child-as-food).
  Function: presented as EVIDENCE; actually decorative/laundered AUTHORITY.
  Witness position: REPRESENTATIVE/INTERPRETIVE (asked to establish a general
    fact about human flesh from hearsay).
  Code: NE1-adjacent / cross-ref SM4, BP6 — the anecdote IS the entire support
    for the scheme's central premise. Operative: the flimsiness is the point.

V3: The "worthy person / true lover of his country" who proposes eating
    teenagers (the venison "refinement"), and the Psalmanazor anecdote about the
    crucified girl of fifteen in "Formosa."
  Attached to: C2/C3 (extending the commodity logic).
  Function: ILLUSTRATION of how far the logic travels; also a satiric jab at
    credulous travel-authority (Psalmanazor was a notorious impostor).
  Witness position: REPRESENTATIVE (hearsay-of-hearsay).
  Code: cross-ref SM4 — mediated authority piled on mediated authority.
    Operative.

V4: The closing self-portrait — "I have no children... the youngest being nine
    years old, and my wife past child-bearing."
  Attached to: the proposer's disinterestedness pose ("no personal interest").
  Function: EMOTIONAL ANCHOR / character seal on the persona.
  Witness position: PARTICIPANT (the proposer vouching his own neutrality).
  Code: PASS as device — the bland personal alibi is the final tonal turn of
    the screw.
```

**NE summary:** No free-floating NE0 decoration — every vignette is attached. The testimonial vignettes (V2, V3) function as laundered authority for the central atrocity (cross-ref SM4/BP6). On a straight read these are NE3 (emotional/rhetorical force masking a logical void); operative, the void is intentional and indicted.

**Codes: SM4/BP6 cross-refs on V2/V3; no NE0.**

## Step 8: Cross-Section Integrity

```
Cross-Section Integrity:
  Claim drift: STABLE. The surface C0 (raise-and-sell children) holds from ¶
    "I shall now therefore humbly propose" to the close. No CL1.
  Qualification erosion: The proposer is OVERCONFIDENT throughout ("not be
    liable to the least objection") — there is no erosion because there were
    no qualifiers to erode. The confidence is uniform and uniformly ironic.
    No FM-A16.
  Scope accumulation: Scope is TIGHTENED, not inflated, at the conclusion
    ("this one individual Kingdom of Ireland, and for no other"). No BP2 /
    no FM-A4. This is the opposite of the usual dynamic failure.
  Definition stability: T2 ("commodity"/"stock") is applied with deliberate,
    escalating consistency — children -> breeders -> carcass -> yearling. The
    category-collision is stable and intensifying by design (CL4-as-device,
    not CL4-as-accident).
  Alternatives gap: NONE — the alternatives are concentrated in the penultimate
    movement and explicitly refused. BP5 does not fire cross-document.
```

**Cross-section verdict:** The dynamic-failure family (FM-A2, FM-A4, FM-A16, FM-A18) largely does NOT fire. The text is *more* disciplined across distance than typical advocacy: stable claim, uniform (ironic) confidence, tightening scope, present alternatives. FM-A18 (Implementation Blindspot) notably does NOT apply — the proposal is grotesquely *over*-implemented (shambles, butchers, recipes, seasons, prices). The one cross-section "failure" that does operate is the sustained CL4 category-smuggle, which is the satire's spine.

## Step 9: Distinguish Protocol

### Decision tests

| Decision Test | Result | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Claim-accessibility | PASS | A careful reader states the surface claim easily; the buried thesis (Ireland's masters already devour the poor; humane remedies exist and are refused) is also recoverable from the text's own framing. |
| Evidence-evaluability | PASS | The reader can identify every support (arithmetic, the American, the advantages list) and assess its adequacy — and finds it deliberately, conspicuously inadequate. |
| Warrant-recoverability | PASS | The load-bearing warrant (treat persons as stock) is stated and rejectable; it is CONTESTED, not MISSING. The bridge is recoverable; the reader recovers it and refuses it. |
| Scope-honesty | PASS | Scope is explicitly declared and even over-restricted; no hidden travel. |
| Objection-awareness | PASS | Alternative positions (the humane expedients) and the strongest objection (cruelty/personhood) are both explicitly present on the page — engaged dishonestly, but present. |
| Form-fit | PASS | The non-standard form (ironic proposer-persona; argument-by-reductio) is doing *real* argumentative work: the reader is forced to supply and reject the monstrous warrant, and that act of refusal is the argument's delivery mechanism. The form is not shielding weakness; the apparent weaknesses ARE the indictment. |

### Classification

**STRUCTURALLY UNCONVENTIONAL BUT EFFECTIVE.**

This is a satirical *reductio ad absurdum* / ironic-persona argument — a recognized non-standard form (argument-by-juxtaposition-and-reversal, kin to prophetic address: it draws the addressed audience into apparent agreement, then reverses to expose complicity). A careful reader can fully recover and test the argument. Every code that fired (CL4, SM4, SM2, WR1, BP4, BP6, OB4, OB2, DI0, DI2) fired because the surface argument *performs* the failure it means to indict. The form does genuine argumentative work; it is not shielding incompetence.

Per the cultural-charity principle and the classification decision rule: **default to Sound/Effective unless a decision test fails as a defeat.** No decision test fails as a defeat — the claim is identifiable, the evidence is assessable, the warrant is recoverable-and-rejectable, scope is honest, objections are visible on the page. The failures are designed soft-surfaces, not structural breaks that block evaluability.

### Charity-gate (Deficit Lock) — legible record of downgrades

The following codes genuinely fired on the surface argument and are **downgraded to advisory (operative-as-device)**, but recorded here so the deficits carry forward legibly (locked at Triage; further softening would require a body override marker):

- **CL4** (definitional smuggling: child -> commodity) — original finding stands; downgraded because the smuggle is made conspicuous *to be rejected*.
- **WR1** (contested warrant, absent backing, on C2) — original finding stands; downgraded because the warrant is recoverable-and-rejectable, not MISSING; the missing backing is the indictment.
- **SM4 / BP6** (laundered/over-burdened testimony, the "American") — stand; downgraded as the device that exposes "projector" credulity.
- **BP4** (false precision theater) — stands; downgraded as parody of political arithmetic.
- **OB4 / DI0 / DI2** (strongest objection conceded without cost; smuggled starting points; pseudo-resolution) — stand; downgraded as the satire's exposure mechanism.

### Retroactive adjustments

No code is *erased*. AT3 is retained as the surface type with the Step-9 note that the operative form is satirical reductio. AC1 (Step 1) is resolved to PASS at operative-audience level. BP5 and OB5 and OB3 are confirmed **not fired** (alternatives present; strongest objection named).
